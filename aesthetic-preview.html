What You Control vs. What You Don't

You control venue selection and accessibility, but not individual attendee travel decisions. These estimates reflect what your venue strategy enables, not what attendees will definitely do.

Leverage Points

  • Venue location is car-dependent. Selecting an urban core venue with transit access could reduce audience travel carbon by 50-70%.
  • Local hiring enables venue-provided equipment use, reducing production freight by 40-60%.

Tradeoffs to Consider

  • Urban core venue: Excellent transit access and low driving, but may have higher venue costs and capacity constraints.
  • Full touring crew: Maximum consistency and control, but highest travel carbon and accommodation costs.

Important: Audience travel is the largest source of uncertainty in event carbon calculations. You control venue selection and accessibility, but not individual travel choices. These estimates reflect what your venue strategy enables, not what attendees will definitely do.
